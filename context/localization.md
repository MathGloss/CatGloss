A **reflective subcategory** of a category $\cC$ is a full subcategory $\cD$ so that the inclusion admits a left adjoint, called the **reflector** or **localization**:\n$$ \xymatrix{ \cD \ar@{^(->}@<-1ex>[r] \ar@{}[r]|-\perp & \cC. \ar@<-1ex>[l]_L}$$
